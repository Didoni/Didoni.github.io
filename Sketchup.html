Sketching ++

A novel and enjoyable sketching experience

This project was my MSc thesis. It dealt with building an interface that allows users to create 3D graphics using Google SketchUp on a multi-view tabletop display developed in the University of Bristol made out of two overlaying screens each streaming a different content. Interfacing it with SketchUp gives users the advantage of seeing different views of the 3D model on the same screen. (you can almost see in the image on the left a rendered see-though ball and it's sharp sketch visible)

Integrating the touchscreen enabled users to draw shapes using their fingers. Viewed from one angle one could see the sketch being drawn, viewed from another - the 3D rendering of that sketch.

Building blocks of the interface

  • Concave and convex
  • Simulate quadratic curved shapes
  • Saddle shapes
  • Simple and Complex Shapes
  • Static and dynamic
  • Maps, pie charts and bar charts
  • Live depth images from a Kinect sensor
